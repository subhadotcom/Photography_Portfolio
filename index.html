About Me

Subhadip Ghosh

I am a passionate wildlife photographer with over 5 years of experience capturing the beauty of nature and wildlife. My work focuses on documenting rare species and their natural habitats.

Through my photography, I aim to raise awareness about wildlife conservation and the importance of preserving our natural ecosystems.

My work has been featured in various wildlife magazines and exhibitions. I regularly conduct photography workshops and tours to share my knowledge and passion with others.
